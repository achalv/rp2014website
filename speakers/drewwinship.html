He represented some of the largest companies in the world in tort, contract, and class action cases. Drew handled lawsuits throughout the Midwest, and is licensed to practice in Missouri, Illinois, and numerous federal courts. In 2012, he founded Juristat. Juristat is "Moneyball" for lawyers. Its proprietary natural language processing algorithm parses public historical legal data and accurately predicts the future behaviors of actors within the legal system. Juristat’s current products focus on the U.S. patent system. Learn how predictive analytics can help improve equity, transparency, and predictability within the system.
